# Claims

This document defines the **explicit and limited claims**
made by this repository.

No claims beyond those listed here are implied.

---

## What is claimed

This repository claims that:

- reasoning behavior can be observed and compared across runs,
- structural invariants in reasoning can be identified,
- drift under repetition can be detected and classified,
- reasoning stability and instability can be documented empirically.

These claims are limited to **observable behavior** only.

---

## What is NOT claimed

This repository does NOT claim:

- correctness of any reasoning outcome,
- optimality of decisions,
- explainability of model internals,
- safety or alignment guarantees,
- general intelligence or capability levels,
- applicability beyond documented cases.

---

## Scope

All claims apply **only** to:

- the specific tasks tested,
- the specific protocol defined in `PROTOCOL.md`,
- the documented runs and evidence.

No extrapolation is implied.
